Welcome to Alina Dayhome

We provide child care for children from 12 months to 6 years old. Our aim is to provide a safe and happy environment where children can grow and learn under the guidance and supervision of our dedicated and caring staff. We hope to fulfill each child physical, intellectual, emotional and social needs, while showing them kindness, consistent discipline, and encouragement in a happy and informal setting.

Our Mission

Alina Dayhome strives to provide a safe and nurturing learning environment for children, staff, and families as demonstrated through professional practice and quality programming.

Culture and Diversity

Our center believes in welcoming every child from different parts of the world to our program and helping them to reach their fullest potential. We aim to include all children in our daily planning through several multicultural activities and strive to break down any barriers so that all may participate.

Our Philosophy

"It takes a community to raise a child". This simple term is used to describe all the joy and importance that children play within our society, as well as the responsibility that comes with raising children. Teamwork is fundamental in raising a child. It is the belief of our program that together as parents and caregivers, we provide an environment that is stimulating, educational, caring, but above all, a place where children feel safe and secure.
